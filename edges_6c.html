6-Color Edge Sequences

After the corners are solved, use the instructions for the 3-color cube to solve the edges. Restore the corners with half-twists and then use the following sequences to complete the solution. You may not always be able to exactly match a setup but you can always find one that will either reduce the number of misplaced facelets (matching 3 of 4 facelets will fix two) or relocate two of them such that a setup can be matched. Some additional tips for solving the Edge Cube are here.


 Sequence 6C-E1
Four misplaced facelets on this cube, the other two are on the back.
Sequence 6C-E2
Four misplaced facelets on this cube, turn to view.
Sequence 6C-E3
Four misplaced facelets on this cube, the other two are on the bottom.
Sequence 6C-E4
Four misplaced facelets on this cube.

All configs in this stage are solvable with half-twists including E2 & E4 above which also use quarter-twists to reduce the number of moves to solve. In this computer-generated list of half-twist only solutions for every possible config it can be seen that E2 (154) takes 12 moves and E4 (13) takes 8 moves (note: the configs are rotated into a different position).
