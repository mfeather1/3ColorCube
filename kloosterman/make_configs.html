Hans Kloosterman's Method
Make Stage 4 Configs

This makes the lists that are used to display the corner and edge configurations in the programs corners.html and edges.html. It can also make the complete list where both corners and edges are scrambled on each cube however the browser cannot display that many configs so the list is reduced by showing only 1 from every 1000 cubes. Either of the "Both" options take a while to run, the progress status can be viewed in the console (F12) which must be open prior to starting to see the status messages. A sample of configs can be selected/copied and displayed here.
