Help

Use the mouse to do half-twists to the cube on the left (or rotate it) to make the setup for Sequence 3C-E1 which is the small cube on the right. The setup cube is uncolored, only the location of the misplaced facelets needs to match on the big cube which gets a different colorscheme on each press of the Random button. If you get stuck press Reset (first green button) and then press Play to see a solution, there is often more than one way to do it.
