4-Color Cube
Corners 3

With solved tri-color corners there are 24 possible configurations of the single-color corners, half with even parity and half with odd parity. The following three sets of four configs are even parity. They are interconnected by half-twists within each set but not across sets (RYGB is the solved state).


RYGB   YRBG   BGYR   GBRY
RBYG   BRGY   GYBR   YGRB
RGBY   GRYB   YBGR   BYRG

These cubes show the above config sets with another config appearing every fourth move.

The parallel sequence is used to go between the three sets.
1-10Parallel Sequence
11-12Solve with half-twists -> RBYG
13-22Parallel Sequence
23-24Solve with half-twists -> RGBY

RBGY   BRYG   YGBR   GYRB
RYBG   YRGB   GBYR   BGRY
RGYB   GRBY   BYGR   YBRG
