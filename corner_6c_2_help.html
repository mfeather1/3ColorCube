This demo shows how to use the 6-Color Corner Sequences to solve the corners of a 6-color cube that is already solved as 3-color. The Waterwheel Sequence is used when only one pair of opposite faces can be solved with half-twists, the setup is having solved faces on the left & right as shown below, all other faces can be ignored. The Parallel Sequence is used when no faces can be solved with half-twists, the setup is parallel vertical pairs on the front face and parallel horizontal pairs on the back face as shown (color of top row on back face must match color of right column on front face), all other faces and centers can be ignored. The sequences do not solve the corners, they make them solvable with half-twists, for more information see above link.
