Use the Supercube Sequences to solve the centers. Above the cube, Center Twist is shown which is the rotation of each center in the order of top, left, front, right, back, bottom. The twist sum is also shown, if it is a multiple of four (0, 4, 8, 12 or 16) then there is a Y indicating that it can be solved without getting the single half-twisted center at the end, otherwise it shows N. Twist values are shown in the following table.


No Twist0
Quarter-Twist Clockwise1
Half-Twist2
Quarter Counter-Clockwise3
