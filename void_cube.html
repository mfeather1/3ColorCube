Void Cube

The Void Cube can be solved normally, however, there are new configurations that do not occur on the regular cube. The following two "parity" configurations are both solved the same way, the slice containing the misplaced facelets is quarter-twisted and then all four edge cubies are flipped by using Sequence 3C-E3 twice. The second cube has the addition of a half-twist before & after. This method is also used for the 4x4 Cube and Magic Cube 4D.

1Quarter-twist the slice
2-8Apply Seqence 3C-E3 to flip two edge cubies
9Turn cube around
10-16Apply Seqence 3C-E3 to flip two edge cubies
